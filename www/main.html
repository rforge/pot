Welcome the Peaks Over Threshold (POT) project!

The POT project aims to provide functional tools to model peaks over (high enough) threshold. These tools are related to the so-called Extreme Value Theory (EVT). In the last decades, the EVT has becomming an increasing popular statistical domain. Typical applications belongs to environmental, insurance and financial analysis. However, still works remain to be done; for instance, spatial modeling for extreme, trend analysis, multivariate extremes.

If you are interested in contributing to the POT project, please do not hesitate to join it!
